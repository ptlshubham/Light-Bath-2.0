Our Story of Evolution

From our humble beginnings as "Parshvanath Sales" in 1998, a trusted retail outlet for agriculture supplies including pipes, water tanks, and pumps, we've embarked on a remarkable journey. In 2015, we proudly joined forces with the prestigious Jaquar Group, a milestone that marked the start of our transformation.

As the years rolled on, we recognized the importance of diversification and the value of offering a wide range of quality products to our valued customers. In 2021, we took another significant step with the launch of "KalpaDrum," becoming a trusted distributor for Astral Pipes LTD., enhancing our commitment to providing top-notch products.

Now, in 2023, we're thrilled to introduce "Light and Bath" a culmination of our continuous efforts to bring the best to our customers. This new endeavor promises an array of premium lighting solutions and bathing essentials, curated with the same dedication to quality and customer satisfaction that has defined our journey thus far.

Thank you for being a part of our evolution. "Light and Bath" is not just a new venture; it's a testament to our dedication to offering excellence, innovation, and a brighter, more comfortable future for you, our valued customers.
